Description

This service enables the individuals to apply for a new Medical healthcare professional license.

Prerequisites

Meeting the minimum requirements.

Documents Required

  • Education certificates with transcript of records. Education Certificates may include any of the followings: Diploma, Bachelor’s, Post Graduates Degree etc.
  • Recent experience letters.
  • Valid License/​Registration from licensing body of country of recent employment.
  • Valid Good Standing Certificate.
  • Valid Passport Copy.
  • Recent Photo.
  • Experience Certificate
  • Offer letter from recruiting facility (If Available).
  • Malpractice Insurance from recruiting facility (If Available).

Fee

Meeting the minimum requirements.

Time Frame

Minimum of 21 Working days. For Urgent requests its reviewed 3 working Days.

Policies & Procedure

License requirements for physicians and dentists Select category. Fill the form. Payment. PLD team review. Issue license.
